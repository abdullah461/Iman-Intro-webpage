Islamic Medical Association of Nigeria, Niger state

Islamic Medical Association of Nigeria is an Islamic, Independent, Professional, Charitable body established to unite all Nigerian Medical/Health professionals and workers and promote a better understanding and appreciation of Islam and of health care within the framework of Islam.

About us

Islamic Medical Association of Nigeria (IMAN) is an umbrella body of all Muslim Health workers in Nigeria, it was first initiated in Makka Saudi Arabia when some group of Muslim Health workers participated in the 1988 Hajj operation. The overall objective is to bring into closer union all Nigeria Muslim Health Care Professionals, to render selfless services to humanity with the fear of Allah (SWT) in mind. IMAN is a member of the Federation of Islamic Medical Associations (FIMA) which served as an international umbrella of all Muslim health workers.

Membership Categories

Ordinary

Any Muslim health care professional or allied worker in the health facilities who subscribes to the spirit of the preamble and abides by the aims and objectives of the Association.

Student

Any Muslim undergraduate student in the medical or health sciences who subscribes to the spirit of the preamble and abides by the aims and objectives of the Association.

Associate

Any other Muslim person approved by the council who subscribes to the spirit of the preamble and abides by the aims and objectives of the Association.
